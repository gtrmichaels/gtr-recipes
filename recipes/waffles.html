Waffles prep

Description

Golden brown and fluffy waffles, perfect for a sweet or savory breakfast.

Ingredients for Waffles:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil

Procedure

  1. Whisk together the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk together the wet ingredients in a separate bowl.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and whisk until just combined.
  4. Heat a waffle iron and grease it with butter or cooking spray.
  5. Pour the batter onto the hot waffle iron and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Serve with your favorite toppings, such as syrup, butter, fruit, or whipped cream.
