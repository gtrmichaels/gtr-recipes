Tacos prep

Description

Indulge in a flavorful explosion of seasoned meat, vegetables, and cheese wrapped in a warm tortilla.

Ingredients for Tacos:

  • 1 pound ground beef or turkey
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 12 corn tortillas
  • Your favorite taco toppings, such as shredded lettuce, cheese, sour cream, and salsa

Procedure

  1. Brown the ground beef or turkey in a large skillet over medium heat. Drain off any excess grease.
  2. Stir in the taco seasoning and water. Bring to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  3. Stir in the onion and bell pepper and cook until softened.
  4. To assemble the tacos, warm the corn tortillas. Place a spoonful of the ground beef mixture in the center of each tortilla. Top with your favorite taco toppings and serve immediately.
